# Plants vs Zombies: Card Battler

A browser-based card battler reimagining of the classic Plants vs Zombies. Build a deck of fantasy plants and spells, place them on a 5×12 grid, and defend the **Aether-Root** against waves of fantasy-themed undead.

## Play the Game

**[▶ Play on GitHub Pages →](https://nors3ai.github.io/Plants-vs-Zombies/)**

> **v1.0.0 is live.** All 12 phases complete. Runs in any modern browser; mobile-friendly.

## Overview

Plants vs Zombies: Card Battler turns the tower-defense classic into a strategic deck-building idler. Spend gold in the shop, place plants and spells on the grid, then watch the round play out automatically. Your only active intervention during combat: casting powerful **Aether-Root spells** from a side panel to keep your Mother Plant alive.

## Key Features

- **Card-Based Gameplay** — Collect Plants and Spells across 6 rarity tiers (Trash → Legendary)
- **5×12 Grid Battlefield** — Strategic placement matters; cards auto-cast once the round begins
- **Card Packs** — Mythic, Arcane, and Frenzy chests with exclusive pack-only cards
- **Idler Combat** — After the 5-second countdown, plants and zombies battle automatically
- **Aether-Root Spells** — Heal, shield, and protect your Mother Plant via the side panel
- **6 Difficulty Modes** — Tutorial, Easy, Normal, Hard, Insane, and unlockable Endless
- **Boss Fights** — Each round ends with a unique fantasy boss with special abilities
- **Economy Plants** — Sunflowers that evolve into Gilded Roses to save board space
- **Endless Mode + Leaderboard** — Unlocked by defeating The Arch-Lich Malakor in Round 10
